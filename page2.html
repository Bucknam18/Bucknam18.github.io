NMDs

NMDs are another one of my favorite pairs of shoes. They have a unique look and are extremely comfortable NMDs are one of Adidas's newest shoes that came out around 2 years ago, NMDs became very popular very quickly and every store ended up selling all different colors of the Adidas NMD shoe These NMDs are from the set of the "Tri Color" Adidas NMDs, They are called "Tri Color" because the three stripes on the shoe are a different color.
